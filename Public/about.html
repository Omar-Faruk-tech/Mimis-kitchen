About the company

Mimi's kitchen is a small and medium enterprise established in the year 2021. Mimi,s kitchen is a subsidiary of Mimi ventures which was also founded aroun the same time by the same person. Mimi's kitchen is currently located at Adegbija crescent, Tanke area of Ilorin south, Ilorin, Kwara State.

About the Chef and CEO

Mimi's kitchen was founded by Miss Adebayo Mistura Olayinka, sole owner and CEO. She finished from university of Ilorin in the year 2019 after which she did her NYSC. She also attained a catering and chef's certificate at Rawfy Cuisine after successful completion of training in 2017.
